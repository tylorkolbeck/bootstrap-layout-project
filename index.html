About Me

Hello, my name is Tylor Kolbeck. I am a 12 year Navy verteran. While in the Navy I was a helicopter crewman. I serverd in multiple roles including instructing and curriculumn development, and flight operations supervisor.

I switched career paths to pursue my passion in web development. Out of high school I started by developing small websites working mostly with HTML, CSS, and PHP. I have spent the past 5 years self learning, honing my skills to be able to call myself a full stack web developer.

I currently work at Jack Henry & Associates as a UI/UX Developer. At JHA I develop and maintain the companies next generation teller software prototype which is used for user testing around the country. On this project I work with the React library and company style frameworks.

I hope to one day take my passion and experience in teaching to teach others web development.
